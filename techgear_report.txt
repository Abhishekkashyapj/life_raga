TechGear Product Catalog - Q4 2025

TechGear is a leading manufacturer of computer peripherals and accessories. Founded in 2018, the company specializes in ergonomic office equipment and high-quality electronics. Their products are designed for professionals who spend long hours at their desks.

Product Categories:

Electronics Division:
TechGear produces a wide range of electronic accessories including wireless mice, USB cables, mechanical keyboards, USB hubs, and screen protectors. The Wireless Mouse series has become their bestseller with over 10,000 units sold quarterly. Their USB-C cables are compatible with all major devices and backed by a 5-year warranty.

Office Equipment:
The Office Equipment line includes monitor stands, desk lamps, monitor risers, laptop stands, and desk organizers. These products are engineered for maximum ergonomics and durability. The Monitor Stand has won multiple design awards for its innovative adjustable design.

Manufacturing:

CableMax specializes in cable manufacturing and produces all USB cables for TechGear. They maintain strict quality control with 99.9% pass rate on all products. Their facility in Taiwan produces over 1 million cables annually.

KeyMaster manufactures mechanical keyboards for TechGear. Known for their premium switches and build quality, KeyMaster keyboards are popular among professional gamers and typists. Each keyboard undergoes 5-point quality testing before shipment.

DeskPro manufactures monitor stands and specialized office furniture. Their engineering team has designed stands that support monitors up to 100 pounds with precision adjustment capabilities.

ErgoPro focuses on ergonomic office equipment including desk lamps and monitor risers. Their products are certified by ergonomic organizations and recommended by occupational therapists.

StandPro produces high-quality laptop stands designed for professionals. Their stands are compatible with all laptop sizes and include cable management features.

ConnectHub manufactures USB hubs with advanced power management. Their hubs support fast charging and data transfer simultaneously.

ScreenGuard produces protective screen films for various devices. Their anti-glare technology reduces eye strain by 40% according to independent testing.

OrganzeMaster specializes in desk organization products made from sustainable materials. Their designs have been featured in office design magazines.

Supply Chain:

TechGear maintains relationships with 8 major manufacturers across Asia. The company has invested heavily in supply chain optimization, reducing lead times from 60 days to just 15 days. Quality control is monitored at each manufacturing partner's facility.

Market Position:

TechGear's products are sold through major retailers including Best Buy, Amazon, and specialty computer stores. The company has expanded to 35 countries in the past 2 years. Their customer satisfaction rating is 4.8 out of 5 stars with over 50,000 customer reviews.

Future Plans:

TechGear plans to launch 5 new product categories in 2026. They are investing in AI-powered ergonomic analysis to create customized product recommendations for customers. The company is also exploring sustainable manufacturing practices with all their partners.
